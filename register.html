Clár Chlainne Uí Chomáin
✦

Dia dhuit — God be with you.

It is my honour to keep this Register at Newhall House, the seat of Clan Ó Comáin in the County of Clare. Inscribed upon these pages are the names of those who, after eight long centuries of silence, have answered the call of the clan and asked to be publicly counted among its number.

Here you will find the Guardians, the Stewards, and the Life Members of Ó Comáin — kindred spread across the wide world, drawn back to one ancient hearth. Their names stand in current standing with the Chief.

Of those at the Clan tier, and any who have asked to remain quietly numbered, no name appears in this place — they are kept upon a separate roll in my own keeping at Newhall, where they are no less honoured for being unseen.
