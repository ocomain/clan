CLAN Ó COMÁIN

Email correspondence — for review

A complete catalogue of the household's automated email sequences, prepared for the Council and the Chief.

What this is. Three email sequences are now in place across the clan's communications infrastructure, totalling eighteen distinct emails. Each is voiced by a specific member of the household — Linda, the Chief, the Herald, Paddy as Seanchaí, Antoin as Tánaiste — and triggered by a particular moment in a member's or prospect's relationship with the clan.

What we ask. Read the emails as the recipient would receive them. Click any title below to view the rendered email exactly as it would land in an inbox. Edits, refinements, and reworkings are welcomed — every email here can be adjusted before any further volume goes out.

One note on the cart re-engagement sequence. Of the three, that sequence is the most recent and has had the least review. The Council's eye on Antoin's voice and Paddy's "is the clan real" framing in particular would be valuable.
