Profile picture

The round emblem

For WhatsApp, Facebook, Messenger — anywhere you have a profile photo.

↓ Download emblem

On an iPhone — WhatsApp

  1. First, tap Download emblem above. The picture saves to your Photos.
  2. Open WhatsApp. Tap Settings at the bottom right.
  3. Tap your name at the very top.
  4. Tap Edit, then Choose Photo, and pick the clan emblem you just saved.
  5. Tap Done. That's it — you're flying the colours.

On an Android phone — WhatsApp

  1. Tap Download emblem above. It saves to your phone.
  2. Open WhatsApp. Tap the three dots at the top right, then Settings.
  3. Tap your name and photo at the top.
  4. Tap the small camera icon, choose Gallery, and pick the emblem.
  5. Tap the tick to save. Done.

On Facebook

  1. Tap Download emblem above to save the picture.
  2. Open Facebook and go to your profile (tap your own photo or name).
  3. Tap your profile picture, then Select Profile Picture.
  4. Choose the clan emblem, then tap Save.
Phone wallpaper

The full crest

The clan arms on a dark background — for the screen you see when you pick up your phone.

↓ iPhone wallpaper ↓ Android wallpaper

On an iPhone

  1. Tap iPhone wallpaper above. The picture saves to your Photos.
  2. Open the Photos app and tap the wallpaper you just saved.
  3. Tap the share button (the square with an arrow going up), at the bottom left.
  4. Scroll down the list and tap Use as Wallpaper.
  5. Tap Add at the top right, then choose Set as Wallpaper Pair. Done.

On an Android phone

  1. Tap Android wallpaper above to save the picture.
  2. Open your Photos or Gallery app and open the saved wallpaper.
  3. Tap the three dots at the top right, then Use as or Set as wallpaper.
  4. Choose Lock screen, Home screen, or both.
  5. Tap Set wallpaper. Done.
Desktop wallpaper

The full crest

For your computer screen — Windows or Mac. Two sizes, to suit your monitor.

↓ Standard (1920×1080) ↓ Large screen (2560×1440)

On Windows

  1. Click a download button above. The picture saves to your Downloads folder.
  2. Open the Downloads folder and find the wallpaper.
  3. Right-click it and choose Set as desktop background.
  4. That's it — the crest is on your screen.

On a Mac

  1. Click a download button above. The picture saves to your Downloads folder.
  2. Find the wallpaper in Downloads and right-click it (or hold Control and click).
  3. Choose Set Desktop Picture.
  4. Done. If you don't see that option, open System Settings → Wallpaper, click Add Photo, and pick the saved file.
Video calls

The call backdrop

A clan backdrop for Zoom and Teams — the crest sits to one side, so it shows over your shoulder. (When your camera is off, the round emblem above makes a fine profile picture too.)

↓ Download backdrop

On Zoom

  1. Click Download backdrop above to save the picture.
  2. Open Zoom. At the bottom left, click the small arrow next to Stop Video.
  3. Choose Choose Virtual Background…
  4. Click the + (or Add Image) and pick the backdrop you saved.
  5. That's it — the crest is behind you on every call.

On Microsoft Teams

  1. Click Download backdrop above to save the picture.
  2. When you join a meeting, click Backgrounds (or More → Effects).
  3. Click Add new and choose the saved backdrop.
  4. Select it, then Apply. Done.
