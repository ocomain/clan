Council broadcasts

Compose a one-off letter from a named Council voice and schedule it to the membership. Stewards and Life Members receive every broadcast 24 hours before the kindred at large — Stewards' Privilege. Markdown supported in the body. Always preview both variants before scheduling, and use the test send if you want to see exactly what arrives.

Compose a broadcast

From: drives the From-address, eyebrow, and signature block.

Shown in the inbox preview and as the email heading.

Markdown: **bold**, *italic*, [link text](url), > quote, blank line separates paragraphs, lines starting "- " become a bulleted list. Portraits: put ![Name](file.jpg) on its own line directly above the paragraph that describes that person — image becomes a round 64px portrait on the left, paragraph runs alongside. Image must be hosted on ocomain.org (just give the filename). Raw HTML is escaped — paste safely. The signature and footer are added automatically.

If you set one you must set both. URL must be http/https/mailto.

Local time. Stewards + Life Members receive at this moment; other members 24 hours later. Must be in the future.

Both previews must be opened at least once before Schedule unlocks.
