Operational tool

Resend publication email

For members whose certificate has been published but whose publication-confirmation email did not reach them. Re-fires the same email, with the same sealed cert PDF attached.

Recipient

Enter the email address on the member's record. The system will look up their published certificate and re-send the publication email to that address.

Must match the email on the member's record exactly.

When to use this

The publication email is sent automatically when a member seals their certificate (either via the dashboard or the auto-publish at day 30). In the rare case that the system reports the cert is sealed but the member did not receive the confirmation email, this tool re-fires the send.

The action is logged in the events table as publication_email_resent with the operator's email, and is safe to re-run if the first attempt fails.
