What they receive

A certificate signed by the Chief — posted to their door

Guardian and Life Member gifts include a formal physical certificate on high-quality stock, bearing the clan crest and the signature of Fergus Kinfauns, The Commane — Chief of Ó Comáin. It is a document designed to be framed and displayed.

Alongside it, the Chief writes a personal letter of welcome — addressing the recipient by name, acknowledging their connection to one of Ireland's oldest Gaelic lineages, and formally entering their name into the Register of Clan Members at Newhall House.

This is not a printed card. It is a document of genuine historical significance — the formal recognition of a person's place in a living Gaelic clan, signed by its Chief.
