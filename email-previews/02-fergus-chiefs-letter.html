Dear Aoife,

A short note to say that my welcome letter to you is attached.

After a long suppression of eight hundred years, Clan Ó Comáin has been brought back to life — and it means a great deal to me that you have taken your place in the founding family.

Please open the letter when you have a quiet moment. It carries the household's seal and is signed by my own hand.

With my warm regards,

Fergus

Fergus Kinfauns, The Commane
Chief of Ó Comáin

Clan Ó Comáin · Newhall House, County Clare, Ireland · www.ocomain.org
