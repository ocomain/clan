A note from the Office

A simple way to grow the clan

Dear Aoife,

A note from the Office, by the Chief's wish, on a matter the clan's revival depends on: bringing more of the kindred home.

There are two simple paths from your members' area:

Invitation — send a short note in your own name. It carries the Chief's letter to your friend or family member. They sign up, and you are recorded as the one who brought them.

Gift — €49 buys someone a Clan Member tier. They receive a signed certificate from the Chief, which can be cherished for years.

Both count toward the clan's honours — Cara at one bringing, Ardchara at five, Onóir at fifteen.

If there is one in your circle who would feel the same call — a parent, a cousin, a friend with Irish roots — five minutes on the dashboard is all it takes.
