How the clan’s honours work

Dear Aoife,

A note from the Office on how the clan's honours work — several members have asked.

The three dignities are simple:

Cara of Ó Comáin — for any member who brings one more into the clan. The vast majority of those raised hold this title.

Ardchara — for those who bring five.

Onóir — the apex, for those who bring fifteen. Very rare. The Onóir bearer is the Chief's named champion of welcome, walking ceremonially beside the Chief and the Privy Council.

A few practical points:

— The dignities are gender-neutral, borne the same by women and men
— Spouses are addressed alongside the bearer by courtesy of the kindred
— Both invitations AND gifts count toward the threshold

If you would like the full ceremonial detail (warrant language, address-formula, etymology), it is all on the honours page in the members' area.

Otherwise: each dignity opens with a single bringing-in, and your members' area is where it is done.
