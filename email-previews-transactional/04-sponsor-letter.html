Ó COMÁIN

A note from Newhall

Through your welcome

Dia dhuit, Antoin — God be with you.

James Brennan has lately taken their place in the Register at Newhall. They came to the clan through your welcome — and their name now stands beside yours among the kindred.

The Chief is grateful for what you bring to the clan. So am I.

— Clan Herald at Newhall

Caithfidh an stair a bheith i réim — History must prevail

Clan Ó Comáin · Newhall House · Co. Clare
