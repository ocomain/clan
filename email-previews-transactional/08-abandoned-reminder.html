We tried to take payment for your membership of Clan Ó Comáin as Guardian of the Clan, but it did not go through — so the payment was not completed and nothing has been charged to your card.

This is usually a small technical matter rather than anything to do with your balance — most often the bank's security check (the one-time code or app approval some cards require) was not completed, or the bank declined an unfamiliar online payment. Your place in the Register is still held; only this last step remains.

You do not need to fill anything in again. The button below takes you straight back to a secure checkout with your details already in place:

Try your payment again

When the bank's security screen appears, do wait for the code or app prompt and approve it — that is the step that completes everything.

If it is declined again, it often helps to use a different card, or to telephone your bank first — a new online payment of this kind sometimes needs their go-ahead. And if any trouble remains, simply reply to this email or write to clan@ocomain.org and the office will see you through it personally.
