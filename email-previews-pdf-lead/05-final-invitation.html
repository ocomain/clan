A note from the Office

The Chief has asked me to write once more

Dear Aoife,

The Chief has asked me to write once more — and for the last time on this matter — in case the moment is now right for you to take a place in the Register.

Membership remains at €49 yearly (€79 family) for the first tier. Upon entry, the Chief will sign and seal your certificate by his own hand, and your name will be inscribed in the Register at Newhall in the form you choose. As 2026 is Year One of the Revival, your record will carry the Founders of the Revival distinction permanently.

Take a place in the Register →

If now is not the moment, I will not write again on this matter. You will continue to receive the occasional Chronicle from us — quarterly news from Newhall — which you can leave at any time with one click.

Whatever you decide, the Chief asked me to thank you for the interest you have taken in the clan.

With kind regards,

Linda Commane Cryan

Private Secretary to the Chief of Ó Comáin
