What happens next

Three things you can expect from this office

i.
A welcome from this office
Within the next few minutes, an email from the Office of the Private Secretary will arrive in your inbox — your formal welcome to the clan, with the details of your membership. (If it has not arrived in 15 minutes, please check your spam folder.)
ii.
A letter from the Chief
In the weeks ahead, Fergus Kinfauns, The Commane, will write to you personally. The Chief writes to every new member of the clan — there is no template, and there is no haste.
iii.
Your place in the Register
Your name has been entered in the Register of Clan Ó Comáin Members at Newhall House. You may now correspond with the clan at any time through this office, at clan@ocomain.org.

As is the custom in the household of the Chief, all correspondence with the clan passes through this office. Direct access to the Chief is reserved — but you have been welcomed in his name, and your name now stands among those of the clan he leads.

Linda Cryan

Office of the Private Secretary to the Chief

Rúnaí Príobháideach an Taoisigh · Newhall House, County Clare
